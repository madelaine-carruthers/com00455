ARTISTS

 

QUEEN

Years Active: 1970 - Present

Queen is a British rock band formed in 1970,including Freddie Mercury, Brian May, John Deacon and Roger Taylor. One of the biggest rock bands of the 70s and 80s, it is the only band to have a song, Bohemian Rhapsody, reach no.1 on the billboard charts three different times. Some of their biggest tracks include We will rock you, Crazy little thing called love, and Radio gaga.

AC/DC

Years Active:1973 - Present

AC/DC is an Australian rock band formed by Scottish-Australian brothers Angus and Malcolm Young in 1973. Until 1980 AC/DC was fronted by Bon Scott, with songs such as Touch Too Much and Highway to Hell. From 1980 the band was fronted by Brian Johnson, with one of the best selling albums of all time, Back in Black. AC/DC is one of Australias biggest musical exports in the rock genre.

GUNS'N'ROSES

Active: 1985 - Present

Guns'n'Roses is a late 80s American rock/metal band, that shot to fame in 1987 for their debut album Appetite for Destruction, which became the best-selling album of all time at its release. The band is acknowledged as one of the biggest rock bands of the 80s and 90s, and a key player in reviving rock music, thanks to their legendary double album, Use Your Illusion I and II.

ELO

Active: 1970–1983, 1985–1986, 2000–2001, 2014–present

ELO is an English rock band fronted by Jeff Lynn, formed in 1970. Most well known for Mr Blue Sky, ELO is a funky mix of rock opera, futuristic jazz, and beatlesque pop. The band has 15 top 20 songs on the US billboard hot 100, and many top 10 albums from the 70s to 80s. ELO's music has become pop culture phenomena, increasingly used in some of the biggest films of the last decade.
