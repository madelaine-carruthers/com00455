ON THIS DAY

The who, what and where of rock and roll history, brought to you today!

 
TODAY August 22

1962 - The Beatles record first TV appearance.

1964 - The Supremes begin two week run at #1 on the US Singles Charts for 'Where did our love go?'.

1978 - Sid Vicious, of The Sex Pistols, gives final live stage performance.

 
YESTERDAY August 21

1961 - The Marvellettes release their first single, 'Please Mr Postman', becoming the groups biggest hit.

1965 - The Rolling Stones begin three week run at #1 on the US album charts with 'Out of our heads'.

1968 - Tommy James and the Shondells return to the #1 position with 'Mony Mony'.
